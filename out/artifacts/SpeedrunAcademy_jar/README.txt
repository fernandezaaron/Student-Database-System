--------------------------------------READ ME-------------------------------------
Speedrun academy is a student grading system that includes 
student portal and admin portal, where in the admin portal the
user/admin is tasked to add students and grade students. As students
are added, student accounts are also created with their student id
and last name. As for the student portal students can view their 
profile and grades that are given to them by the administrator.
